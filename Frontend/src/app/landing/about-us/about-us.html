About Sendly

Connecting people and businesses with speed and reliability.

Our Mission

At Sendly, our mission is to redefine the delivery experience. We believe that sending and receiving parcels should be simple, seamless, and stress-free. We are committed to leveraging advanced technology and a customer-centric approach to provide a service that is not just fast, but also reliable and secure.

Our Story

Founded with a passion for logistics and a desire to bridge distances, Sendly started as a small team with a big vision. We saw the need for a delivery service that truly cares about its customers' packages. Today, we have grown into a trusted partner for thousands of individuals and businesses, but our core values remain the same: integrity, efficiency, and a relentless focus on customer satisfaction.
